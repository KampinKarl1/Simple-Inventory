The camera is supposed to move like the Scene view camera. 

Click to move the player (The gray bean).

Get within distance of a mineable (tree or bush) and right click to mine it.

Press tab to open the crafting and inventory panels. The crafting table will update as you get the ingredients necessary to create objects.
